Our Services

Comprehensive bioinformatics solutions for research and clinical applications

Genomic Analysis

Comprehensive whole-genome and exome sequencing with advanced variant calling and annotation pipelines for research and clinical diagnostics.

Data Integration

Multi-omics data integration combining genomics, transcriptomics, and proteomics for comprehensive biological insights and systems-level understanding.

Drug Discovery

Biomarker Discovery

Statistical analysis and machine learning approaches for identifying diagnostic, prognostic, and predictive biomarkers across diseases.

Clinical Genomics

Pipeline Development
